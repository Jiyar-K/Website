Erbil Citadel Since 5000 Years

The Erbil Citadel, located in the heart of Erbil, Iraq, stands as a testament to centuries of human civilization. Its ancient walls, dating back over 5000 years, encapsulate the rich history and cultural heritage of the region. As one of the oldest continuously inhabited settlements in the world.
